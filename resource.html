  • Spatial-Deconv Tool
    一套用于解析复杂组织中空间分辨率数据并整合病原体载量的开源软件。 An open-source software suite for resolving spatial resolution data in complex tissues and integrating pathogen load.

    专门针对受病原体感染样本的高分辨率空间文库构建标准流程。 Standard workflow for high-resolution spatial library construction specifically for samples infected with pathogens.
