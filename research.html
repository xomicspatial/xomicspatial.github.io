主要研究领域 Primary Research Areas

Direction 01

基于测序微米级芯片的空间组学技术开发 Development of Spatial Omics via Micron-scale Sequencing Chips

开发基于微米级测序芯片的通用检测平台，实现亚细胞分辨率下空间多组学（蛋白质、RNA修饰、DNA修饰）的同步分析，突破传统组学技术无法捕捉空间位置信息的局限。 Developing universal detection platforms based on micron-scale sequencing chips to enable simultaneous spatial multi-omics analysis (proteins, RNA modifications, DNA modifications) at subcellular resolution, breaking the limitations of traditional omics in capturing spatial information.
Direction 02

疫源动物生物学的空间组学研究 Spatial Omics in Reservoir Animal Biology

应用高分辨率空间组学技术，解析疫源动物（如蝙蝠、啮齿类）的病原体-宿主互作机制，精确定位病原体分布、靶细胞及免疫应答的空间特征，为疫病防控提供理论支撑。 Applying high-resolution spatial omics to decode pathogen-host interaction mechanisms in reservoir animals (e.g., bats, rodents). We precisely map pathogen distribution, target cells, and spatial immune response characteristics to provide theoretical support for disease prevention and control.
Direction 03

疫源动物抗体表位的高通量解析与预测 High-throughput Analysis and Prediction of Antibody Epitopes

发展高通量抗体解析技术，整合BCR/TCR测序与AI模型，追溯疫源动物的病原暴露史，预测关键抗原表位，评估跨物种传播风险，助力新型疫苗研发。 Developing high-throughput antibody analysis techniques by integrating BCR/TCR sequencing with AI models. This allows us to trace pathogen exposure history in reservoir animals, predict key antigenic epitopes, and assess cross-species transmission risks to aid in vaccine development.
